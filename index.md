  ## About Me     

  I am a PhD candidate in **Mechanical Engineering** at **Iowa State
  University** (expected 2027),
  working in the Sustainable Energy Systems Analysis Group under the supervision
   of
  [Dr. Mark Mba-Wright](https://www.engineering.iastate.edu/). My research sits
  at the intersection
  of computational modeling, sustainability science, and data-driven
  decision-making — with a focus
  on making renewable energy and biorefinery systems more efficient, equitable,
  and economically viable.

  I hold an **MS in Computer Science and Engineering** from St. Petersburg
  Electrotechnical University
  'LETI' (Russia, 2024) and a **BEng in Mechanical Engineering** from the
  University of Agriculture,
  Makurdi (Nigeria, 2020). I maintain a 4.0/4.0 GPA in my doctoral program.
